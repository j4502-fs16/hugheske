Katie Hughes
Multimedia Producer
My name is Katie Hughes and I am from the suburbs of St. Louis, Missouri. I am currently attending the University of Missouri – Columbia School of Journalism. I’ve known since high school that I love telling stories through multimedia. Ever since I knew how to write and doodle, I’ve been creating my own stories. I’ve worked with programs as simple as Windows Movie Maker up to Adobe Premiere Pro and Final Cut Pro. My goal is have a career in multimedia production, which, to me, is not only to piecing together stories, but works of art.
